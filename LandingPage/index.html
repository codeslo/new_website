Teaching the Central Coast to code.

CodeSLO is a community coding school that offers classes and meetups for people who want to learn computer programming. Our primary focus is on web development using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.

Services

Community

We teach people to code in a way that is accessible and nurturing. We are inclusive and encouraging, and we inspire people to tackle hard challenges.

Educational Organizations

We provide remote programs that are bootcamp-style coding classes for community colleges, apprenticeship programs, and other educational groups.

Businesses

We produce job-ready applicants
